Lamb Chops

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)

Instructions to follow

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Season the lamb chops generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  • In a skillet or oven-safe pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  • Sear the lamb chops for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden-brown crust.
  • Add minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and thyme to the pan, and cook for another minute, allowing the flavors to infuse into the meat.
  • Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast the lamb chops for 8-10 minutes for medium-rare, or adjust the time based on your desired level of doneness.
  • If using, add a tablespoon of butter to the pan during the last few minutes of cooking, basting the lamb chops with the melted butter.
  • Remove the lamb chops from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  • Serve the succulent lamb chops hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes such as roasted vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad.
