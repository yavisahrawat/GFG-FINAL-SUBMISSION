Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless, sliced chicken thighs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp each: cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric
  • 1 tsp cinnamon, cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Pita bread
  • Sliced tomatoes, cucumbers
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Red onion slices

Estimated cost

  • The Estimated Budget would be :- 200-350 rupees

Instructions to follow

  • Marinate Chicken
  • Mix chicken with all marinade ingredients. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours. Cook Chicken
  • Grill or cook marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly charred. Assemble Shawarma
  • Warm pita bread, spread garlic sauce, add chicken, tomatoes, cucumbers, lettuce, and red onions.
  • Serve
  • Roll and enjoy your quick and delicious homemade chicken shawarma!
