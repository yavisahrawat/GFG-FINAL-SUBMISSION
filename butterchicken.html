Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup soaked cashew nuts
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions to follow

  • Combine 500g cubed chicken with 1 cup yogurt, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp garam masala, and salt. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  • Thread marinated chicken onto skewers and bake at 200°C for 15-20 mins.
  • In a pan, heat 2 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil. Sauté 1 finely chopped onion. Add 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, and cook until the onion is golden. Stir in 1 cup tomato puree, cook until the oil separates.
  • Blend 1/2 cup soaked cashew nuts into a paste. Add it to the tomato mixture.
  • Stir in 1 cup heavy cream, 1 tsp garam masala, and salt. Simmer for 5-7 mins until the sauce thickens.
  • Add the baked chicken to the sauce. Simmer for an additional 10 mins.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve the butter chicken hot.
