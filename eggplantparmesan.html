Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • Salt, for sweating the eggplant
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Olive oil, for frying

Instructions to follow

  • Sprinkle the eggplant slices with salt and let them sit for 30 minutes to draw out the bitterness. Rinse and pat dry with paper towels.
  • Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  • Dredge the eggplant slices in the flour, then dip them in the egg, and coat them with the breadcrumb mixture.
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the breaded eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce, then arrange a layer of fried eggplant slices. Top with more sauce, followed by slices of fresh mozzarella. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of marinara and mozzarella on top.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  • Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
