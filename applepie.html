Apple Pie

Ingredients for the Pie Crust

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Ingredients for the Apple Filling

  • 6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples (a mix of tart and sweet varieties like Granny Smith and Honey crisp works well)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, cut into small pieces

Instructions to follow

  • To make the pie crust, mix the flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. Add the chilled butter pieces and blend with a pastry cutter or fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  • Gradually add the ice water, a little at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Divide the dough in half, shape into discs, and wrap each in plastic wrap.
  • Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  • Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • On a floured surface, roll out one disc of dough to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the rolled-out dough into the pie dish and trim the edges.
  • In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.
  • Pour the apple mixture into the prepared pie crust. Dot the top of the apples with small pieces of butter.
  • Roll out the second disc of dough and place it over the apples. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal the pie.
  • Make a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking.
  • Optionally, brush the top crust with an egg wash (1 beaten egg mixed with a tablespoon of water) for a golden finish.
  • Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  • Allow the pie to cool before serving. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
