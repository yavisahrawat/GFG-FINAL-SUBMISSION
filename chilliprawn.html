Chilli Prawn

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) large prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced
  • 2-3 fresh red chilies, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • Fresh cilantro or green onions for garnish

Instructions to follow

  • In a bowl, toss the prawns with cornstarch, salt, and black pepper until well coated.
  • Heat the cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the prawns and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the prawns from the pan and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Sauté the minced garlic and ginger until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  • Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and red chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  • In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, tomato ketchup, sweet chili sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and chicken or vegetable broth.
  • Pour the sauce mixture into the pan with the vegetables and bring to a simmer.
  • Add the cooked prawns back into the pan and toss everything together until the prawns are coated with the sauce.
  • Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the prawns are heated through and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions.
  • Serve the delicious chili prawns hot with steamed rice or noodles for a delightful and flavorful meal! Adjust the amount of red chilies according to your spice preference.
