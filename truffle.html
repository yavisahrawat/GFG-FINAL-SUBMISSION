Truffle

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (30g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons truffle oil (optional)

Instructions to follow

  • Preheat Oven
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a cake pan. Melt Chocolate and Butter
  • In a bowl, melt chocolate and butter together. Allow it to cool slightly. Mix Wet Ingredients
  • Whisk sugar and eggs until light and fluffy. Add vanilla extract and truffle oil. Combine Dry Ingredients
  • In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Combine Mixtures
  • Gradually add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture. Fold in the dry ingredients until just combined. Bake
  • Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs. Cool and Serve
  • Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack. Serve when completely cooled.
