Lobster Thermidor

Ingredients

  • 2 cooked lobsters, meat removed from the shells and chopped
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyere cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup fresh breadcrumbs

Instructions to follow

  • In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for a few minutes.
  • Gradually whisk in the milk and cook until the sauce thickens.
  • Add the Gruyere and Parmesan cheeses, white wine, Dijon mustard, and parsley. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Stir in the chopped lobster meat, making sure it is well-coated with the sauce.
  • Spoon the mixture back into the lobster shells or into a baking dish.
  • Sprinkle the top with fresh breadcrumbs.
  • Broil for a few minutes until the breadcrumbs are golden and the sauce is bubbly.
