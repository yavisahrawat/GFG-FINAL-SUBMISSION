Veg Kohlapuri

Ingredients

  • cashews 6-7 nos
  • cloves 2-3 nos
  • small piece of cinnamon 1 nos
  • pieces of cardamom 2 nos
  • jeera( cumin seeds) 1 tsp
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • cloves of garlic 4 nos
  • onions 2 nos
  • tomatoes 2 nos
  • chopped capsicum 1 bowls
  • mix veggies chopped( carrot, cauliflower,green peas and french beans) 2 bowls
  • milk 2 cups
  • oil 4 tsp
  • turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • chilli powder 1 tsp
  • garam masala 1/2 tsp
  • Salt as per taste 1 tsp
  • kasuri methi 1 pinch
  • corriander 1 tsp
  • grated cheese 3 tsp

Instructions to follow

  • Grind the cashews cloves,cinnamom and cardamom
  • To this powder add garlic,onions and tomatoes and grind to make a smooth paste
  • Heat 4 teaspoons of oil
  • Add the paste/ gravy
  • Add 1 bowl of capsicum
  • Add the mix veggies
  • Add 2 cups of milk
  • Add salt,turmeric, chilli powder and garam masala powder
  • Cover and cook for 15 minutes on low flame
  • Add a pinch of kasuri methi and cook for 2 minutes
  • Garnish with corriander .
  • Add grated cheese to make it yummier
