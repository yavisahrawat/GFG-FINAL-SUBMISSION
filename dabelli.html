Dabelli

Ingredients for the Potato Fillings

  • 4 medium-sized boiled potatoes, peeled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup fresh pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tablespoons Dabeli masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind-date chutney
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • Water as needed

For Assembling the Dabelli

  • 8 Dabeli buns or pav
  • Tamarind-date chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Sev (crispy fried gram flour vermicelli)
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Finely chopped onions
  • Finely chopped coriander leaves
  • Butter for toasting the buns

Instructions to follow

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, and when they crackle, add asafoetida and curry leaves.
  • Saute the chopped onions until they turn translucent.
  • Add the mashed potatoes, Dabeli masala, salt, and a little water. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add roasted peanuts, pomegranate seeds, tamarind-date chutney, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix everything thoroughly and cook for another 2 minutes. The potato filling is ready.
  • Slice the Dabeli buns or pav horizontally without cutting them all the way through.
  • Apply tamarind-date chutney on one side and green chutney on the other side of the buns.
  • Stuff the prepared potato filling inside the buns.
  • Heat a pan or griddle and spread some butter on it. Toast the stuffed buns on both sides until they turn light golden and crispy.
  • Once done, remove from the heat and garnish with sev, pomegranate seeds, finely chopped onions, and coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with extra chutneys and garnishes on the side.
