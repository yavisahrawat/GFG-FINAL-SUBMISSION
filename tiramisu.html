Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks.
  • 3/4 granulated sugar.
  • 2/3 cup mascarpone cheese.
  • 1 cup heavy cream.
  • 1+1/2 strong brewed cup of coffee.
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liquor.
  • 24 to 30 breadsticks.
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder.

Instructions to follow

  • In a large mixing bowl, using a hand mixer or stand mixer, beatthe egg yolks and sugar together until the mixture is thick and pale yellow.
  • Add the mascarpone cheese to the egg yolk mixture and beat until smooth.
  • In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  • Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined. Set the mixture aside.
  • In a shallow dish, combine the cooled coffee and coffee liqueur, if using.
  • Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are soaked but not overly saturated. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) dish or a similar-sized serving dish.
  • Spread half of the mascarpone mixture evenly over the breadsticks.
  • Create another layer of dipped ladyfingers on top of the mascarpone layer, followed by the remaining mascarpone mixture on top.
  • Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least hours or preferably overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.
  • Before serving, dust the top with a generous amount of unsweetened cocoa powder using a fine-mesh sieve.
  • Slice and serve chilled.
