Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) of fish fillets (such as cod, tilapia, or salmon), cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 or 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  • 1 teaspoon tamarind paste or lemon juice (optional for a tangy flavor)

Instructions to follow

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute or until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy and the oil starts to separate.
  • Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  • Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine all the ingredients.
  • Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.
  • Add the fish chunks to the curry and gently mix them with the gravy.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and salt according to your taste. Stir gently to combine without breaking the fish.
  • If you want a tangy flavor, add tamarind paste or lemon juice at this stage.
  • Cover the pan and let the fish cook in the curry for 8-10 minutes on medium-low heat, or until the fish is fully cooked.
  • Once the fish is cooked, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve the fish curry hot with steamed rice or your choice of bread.

    • Feel free to adjust the spices and seasonings to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy the flavorful fish curry as a hearty and comforting meal!
